## HJ Shipbuilding & Construction — Business Overview

HJ Shipbuilding & Construction operates two main business segments: **Shipbuilding** and **Construction**.

### Shipbuilding

1. **Special Vessels (Defense)** — Naval ships and government vessels
   - Ministry of Defense orders: Large transport ships (LPX), large landing ships (LST), high-speed patrol boats (PKX-B), high-speed landing craft (LSF-II), etc.
   - Government procurement: Coast guard patrol ships, icebreaker research vessels, geophysical research vessels, eco-friendly fishery patrol ships, etc.

2. **Commercial Shipbuilding**
   - International orders: Tankers, container ships, bulk carriers, gas carriers, etc.

3. **Other**
   - Ship repair and maintenance

### Construction

1. **Building Construction** — Airports, railway stations, terminals, offices, logistics facilities, exhibition halls
2. **Residential** — Apartment construction, redevelopment/reconstruction projects
3. **Civil Engineering / Overseas** — Roads, railways, subways, ports, runways, and other infrastructure
4. **Plant Engineering** — Power generation facilities, desulfurization equipment, fueling facilities, cranes, and industrial equipment
